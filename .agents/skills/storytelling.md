# Storytelling Skill for AI Agents

## Overview
This skill enhances the AI's ability to create compelling narratives, develop deep characters, and maintain consistent plot arcs.

## Key Techniques
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Describe emotions and events through actions and sensory details.
- **Pacing**: Vary sentence length and chapter structure to control the flow of the story.
- **Character Consistency**: Maintain character motivations and speech patterns throughout the novel.
- **World-Building**: Seamlessly integrate background lore without resorting to "info-dumps."

## Usage
- Apply these principles when generating new chapters or refining existing text.
- Use the context from previous chapters and chat history to ensure narrative continuity.
